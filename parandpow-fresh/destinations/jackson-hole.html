Jackson Hole has the greatest vertical drop of any US ski resort and some of the most challenging terrain in North America. Corbet's Couloir is the most famous expert run in American skiing. The town of Jackson is charming, the wildlife viewing is world-class, and golf in the shadow of the Tetons is a genuinely unforgettable experience.

TRIP PLANNING

Fast Facts

Where to stay: Four Seasons Jackson Hole, Amangani, or Hotel Jackson

Getting there: Jackson Hole Airport (JAC) — 20 min drive, most scenic airport approach in the USA

Budget: $3,000–7,000 per person for 5 days

INSIDER TIPS

  • Tram to the top is mandatory on your first day
  • Apres ski at the Mangy Moose is a Jackson institution
  • Grand Teton National Park is 10 minutes away — don't skip it
  • Snowmobiling in Yellowstone is an extraordinary winter addition
