Lake Louise is one of the most visually stunning ski resorts in the world — the frozen lake, the Fairmont Chateau, and the Victoria Glacier provide a backdrop that photographers chase. Banff Springs Golf Course, designed by Stanley Thompson, is routinely listed among the top 100 golf courses in the world. The combination of UNESCO World Heritage landscape, world-class skiing, and legendary golf makes this one of the great dual-sport destinations on earth.

TRIP PLANNING

Fast Facts

Where to stay: Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise, Fairmont Banff Springs, or Buffalo Mountain Lodge

Getting there: Calgary International (YYC) — 90 min drive on the Trans-Canada Highway

Budget: CAD $3,500–7,000 per person for 5 days

INSIDER TIPS

  • Ski Lake Louise, Sunshine Village, and Norquay on a SkiBig3 pass
  • Wildlife is everywhere — elk and deer frequently cross ski runs
  • The Fairmont hotels are expensive but the experience is worth it once
  • Banff town has excellent dining and nightlife for a mountain town
