Fast Facts

Where to stay: Fairmont Tremblant, Le Westin Resort, or Tremblant village condos

Getting there: Montreal (YUL) — 2 hour drive north

Budget: CAD $2,500–5,000 per person for 5 days

INSIDER TIPS

  • Le P'tit Train du Nord rail trail is spectacular in fall and winter
  • Jouvence spa after skiing is a Quebec tradition
  • Learn a few French phrases — locals appreciate the effort
  • Golf Tremblant has two championship courses
