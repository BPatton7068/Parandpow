The spiritual home of alpinism and extreme skiing. The Vallée Blanche — a 20km off-piste descent — is one of the great ski experiences in the world. Golf Club Chamonix sits in the valley with views of Mont Blanc, Europe's highest peak.

TRIP PLANNING

Fast Facts

Where to stay: Hameau Albert 1er, Grand Hotel des Alpes, Hotel Mont-Blanc

Getting there: Geneva (GVA) — 1 hour drive

Budget: EUR 3,000–8,000 per person for 5 days

INSIDER TIPS

  • The Vallée Blanche requires a guide for first-timers
  • Aiguille du Midi cable car is the most dramatic lift in the world
  • Chamonix town is genuinely French — no resort artificiality
  • Les Grands Montets is best for advanced freeriders
