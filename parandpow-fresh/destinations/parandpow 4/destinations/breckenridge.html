TRIP PLANNING

Fast Facts

Where to stay: The Village Hotel, One Ski Hill Place, Grand Colorado on Peak 8

Getting there: Denver (DEN) — 1.5 hour drive on I-70

Budget: $2,000–4,000 per person for 5 days

INSIDER TIPS

  • Peak 6 is the newest and least crowded peak
  • The historic Main Street has the best bar scene in Colorado skiing
  • Altitude hits hard at 9,600 feet — hydrate aggressively
  • Golf at 9,300 feet means the ball flies significantly farther
