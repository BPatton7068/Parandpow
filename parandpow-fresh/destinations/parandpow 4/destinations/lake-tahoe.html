Lake Tahoe is the only year-round dual-sport destination in the continental USA. Palisades Tahoe hosted the 1960 Winter Olympics. Edgewood Tahoe is a stunning lakeside golf course that has hosted PGA Tour events.

TRIP PLANNING

Fast Facts

Where to stay: Resort at Squaw Creek, Edgewood Tahoe Resort

Getting there: Reno-Tahoe (RNO) — 45 min, or Sacramento (SMF) — 2 hours

Budget: $2,500–5,000 per person for 5 days

INSIDER TIPS

  • March and April offer the best spring skiing
  • Book Edgewood Tahoe early — fills up fast in summer
  • Heavenly has the best lake views
  • South Shore has better nightlife, North Shore is quieter
