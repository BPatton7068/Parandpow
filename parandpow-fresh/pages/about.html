WHY PAR & POW EXISTS

Par & Pow was built by someone who has been snowboarding and playing golf seriously for over 30 years — and got tired of the fact that nobody was talking to people who do both.

The overlap is obvious if you've lived it. The same person who drops $4,000 on a golf trip to Scotland is the same person who blows a long weekend at Whistler. They just haven't figured out yet that they can do both on the same trip — or that there are destinations where you genuinely don't have to choose.

That's what Par & Pow is. Not a travel agency. Not a gear blog run by people who've never used the equipment. Just honest, first-hand intel on where to go, when to go, and how to make the most of every trip — from someone who has stood on the tee box at 7am and been on snow by 2pm.

Every destination on this site has been researched with real riders and golfers in mind. Every gear review reflects actual time with the equipment. Every trip guide is built around the reality that you want to do both sports properly — not just check both boxes.

This site is the guide we wish existed when we started doing this. We hope it makes your trips better.
